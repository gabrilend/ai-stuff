## Tier 1: Core Themes (10 Primary Categories) - Final

### resistance
- **Description:** Anti-authoritarian sentiment, revolutionary politics, and systematic critique of power structures
- **Keywords:** revolution, fascism, capitalism, power, authority, organizing, fight, resistance, collective, liberation, struggle, protest, anarchist, leftist, solidarity, uprising, defiance, system-breaking, opposition, militant
- **Prevalence:** 35%
- **Visual Style:** Bold reds and blacks, sharp angular forms, broken chains, jagged lightning patterns

### technology  
- **Description:** Deep technical knowledge mixed with philosophical questioning of digital systems
- **Keywords:** programming, algorithms, AI, code, software, linux, systems, networks, automation, debugging, data, encryption, terminal, computers, digital, infrastructure
- **Prevalence:** 30%
- **Visual Style:** Electric blues and greens, circuit board layouts, binary cascades, network topologies

### isolation
- **Description:** Profound loneliness and social disconnection despite digital connectivity  
- **Keywords:** alone, lonely, disconnected, misunderstood, withdrawn, separated, alienation, distance, silence, empty, abandoned, solitary, exile, hermit
- **Prevalence:** 25%
- **Visual Style:** Muted grays and blues, sparse compositions, vast negative space, isolated cells

### identity
- **Description:** Fluid exploration of gender, sexuality, neurodivergence, and authentic selfhood
- **Keywords:** trans, gender, autism, ADHD, neurodivergent, queer, witch, authentic, transformation, mask, performance, binary, spectrum, fluid
- **Prevalence:** 20%
- **Visual Style:** Prismatic refractions, rainbow spectrums, morphing shapes, kaleidoscope effects

### systems
- **Description:** Analysis of complex organizational patterns from economic to computational architectures
- **Keywords:** systems, structure, organization, mechanics, dynamics, infrastructure, architecture, design, framework, process, balance, distributed
- **Prevalence:** 18%
- **Visual Style:** Blueprint blues, network diagrams, hierarchical trees, grid systems, modular components

### connection
- **Description:** Yearning for authentic human bonds contrasted against digital isolation
- **Keywords:** friends, community, friendship, belonging, trust, communication, empathy, understanding, neighbors, solidarity, collective, cooperation
- **Prevalence:** 15%
- **Visual Style:** Warm oranges and yellows, interconnected webs, flowing connections, bridge structures

### chaos
- **Description:** Stream-of-consciousness fragmentation and breakdown of systematic thinking
- **Keywords:** stack-overflow, fragments, broken, scattered, interrupted, glitch, random, confusion, noise, overwhelm, chaos, manic
- **Prevalence:** 12%
- **Visual Style:** Glitch aesthetics, RGB separation, broken grids, fragmented compositions

### transcendence
- **Description:** Mystical and spiritual exploration combining witchcraft with cosmic consciousness
- **Keywords:** witch, magic, divine, spiritual, mystical, gods, spirits, transcendent, cosmic, sacred, ritual, prophecy, ethereal
- **Prevalence:** 10%
- **Visual Style:** Deep purples and golds, sacred geometry, mandala forms, spiral galaxies

### survival
- **Description:** Practical concerns about basic needs and resource management under economic precarity
- **Keywords:** food, water, shelter, resources, money, rent, broke, survival, scarcity, basic-needs, practical, preparation
- **Prevalence:** 8%

### creativity

- **Prevalence:** 5%

function generate_pdf_art(text_passage)
