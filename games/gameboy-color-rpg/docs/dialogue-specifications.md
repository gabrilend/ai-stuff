# Dialogue Specifications - Game Boy Color RPG
*Caravan Journey Text Content Guidelines*

**Technical Constraints:**
- Maximum 32 characters per message
- Maximum 16 characters per line
- Two lines maximum per dialogue box

**AI Generation Hints:**
- Keep language simple and direct
- Use medieval/fantasy vocabulary sparingly
- Focus on immediate, practical concerns
- Reflect caravan lifestyle and travel themes

## WORLD DESIGN SETTINGS
*AI Generation Guidelines for Location-Specific Dialogue*

### Haunted Woods Camp
*Generate dialogue reflecting eerie atmosphere and supernatural dread for this location.*

- Ancient twisted trees groan in wind, casting dancing shadows
- Locals whisper of cursed ground where travelers vanish
- Strange howls echo through darkness, defying explanation
- Camp positioned safely beyond woods but still touched by fear

### Desert Oasis Rest  
*Generate dialogue emphasizing relief, trade, and desert survival themes.*

- Precious water source surrounded by life-giving palm trees
- Merchant crossroads where distant cultures meet and trade
- Mirages trick the eye while sandstorms threaten approach
- Refuge from scorching heat offers brief respite to weary

### Mountain Pass Shelter
*Generate dialogue focused on harsh conditions and alpine challenges.*

- Thin mountain air makes breathing difficult, cold bites deep
- Avalanche dangers and rockslides threaten narrow paths
- Stunning vistas reward those brave enough to climb high
- Mining settlements carved into stone hide ancient secrets

### Abandoned Village Stop
*Generate dialogue exploring mystery and melancholy of empty places.*

- Empty houses stand silent with doors creaking in wind
- Overgrown streets tell stories of sudden mysterious departure
- Nature slowly reclaims what civilization once built
- Valuable supplies remain but ghost stories discourage looting

### Riverside Market Town
*Generate dialogue celebrating commerce, fertility, and water's gifts.*

- Swift flowing river brings life to fertile surrounding lands
- Busy bridge crossing charges tolls but offers safe passage
- Fish markets bustle while water mills grind grain steadily
- Spring floods threaten but also renew the rich soil

### Ancient Ruins Campsite
*Generate dialogue mixing wonder, danger, and archaeological intrigue.*

- Crumbling stone architecture hints at forgotten civilizations
- Magical energies still pulse through weathered ancient walls
- Treasure hunters compete with scholars studying mysterious symbols
- Guardian spirits protect secrets hidden in shadowed passages
